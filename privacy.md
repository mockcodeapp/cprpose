Privacy Policy for CPRpose
At CPRpose, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing high-quality educational tools for CPR training. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.
1. Data Collection and Usage
Our app uses your device’s camera to perform real-time pose estimation to help you practice CPR techniques on a manikin.
 No Data Storage or Transmission: All pose estimation processing is performed locally on your device using Apple frameworks and MediaPipe.
 No Image/Video Retention: We do not capture, save, store, or transmit any photos, video feeds, or biometric facial/body data to any external servers. The visual data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded by the app.
 Permissions: The app requests camera access solely for the purpose of providing the educational feedback features described within the app.
2. Third-Party Services
We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or visual data.
3. Children's Privacy
CPRpose is designed for educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
4. Security
Because we do not store your data on external servers, the risk of data breaches involving user information is minimized. All processing occurs strictly within the sandbox of your device.
5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
6. Contact Us
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: mockcode.app@gmail.com
